3. Feedzy (For Fetching Content From RSS Feeds)

Feedzy is a popular RSS feed plugin that lets you fetch content from different RSS feeds and display them on your website.

With this plugin, you can collect fresh news content from unlimited RSS feeds via bulk import. Once you do that, the plugin will convert the data into WordPress posts, pages, and custom post types.

You can then edit the content, add or remove feature images, and then publish the news articles on your WordPress blog. You are also able to add social sharing buttons to these blog posts so that users can share the content with their followers.

Pros

  • With Feedzy, you can create a news ticker that scrolls across your website and provides the latest news to users.
  • The plugin can be used to create a newsletter signup form.
  • Feedzy can aggregate RSS feeds from a variety of sources and uses shortcodes to add feeds to posts or pages.
  • It sends you an email notification when new articles are published by one of the websites that you have subscribed to.

Cons

  • Feedzy relies on RSS feeds to collect content, restricting the range of news sources you can aggregate.
  • Its free plan has limited features.
  • Feedzy cannot handle dynamic content like live updates or real-time data feeds effectively.

Why we recommend Feedzy: If you want to add news content to your site using RSS feeds from other sources, then Feedzy is the best choice. It also lets you add newsletter signup forms and a news ticker to show the latest news to your site visitors.

6. PublishPress (For Managing the Editorial Workflow)

PublishPress is a suite of WordPress plugins that help manage the editorial workflow of your news website.

The plugin lets you create custom post types for news articles, categories, and tags and comes with an editorial calendar to provide an overview of the publishing schedule.

If you have multiple authors on your news site, then you can also use PublishPress for access control, media library permissions, navigation menu restrictions, and to hide admin area menu tabs.

Pros

  • You can create reusable content templates, schedule your news articles to be automatically published, and track your content performance with the plugin.
  • It lets you manage multiple WordPress sites from the same dashboard.
  • PublishPress Series lets you create magazines and newsletters with multiple articles and also groups similar content to create a series.
  • Its widget lets you display a series of news articles together.

Cons

  • The advanced features of the plugin are only in the paid plan.
  • PublishPress does not let you create a news ticker or newsletter form.

Why we recommend PublishPress: We recommend using this plugin to manage the editorial workflow of your news website. It comes with a content calendar, widgets for displaying news articles, and lets you schedule your posts to be published automatically.

TopNewsWP also offers other features like zooming in on news images, controlling the news title length, and displaying sources for each news article.
