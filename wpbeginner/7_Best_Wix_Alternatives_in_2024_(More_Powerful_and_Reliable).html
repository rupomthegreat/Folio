Are you looking for the best Wix alternatives?

Wix is a website builder that people use to create a website when they first start out, likely because they saw a TV commercial or heard one of the many ads.

The challenge is that their free version is quite limited in functionality, and it gets really expensive when you try to upgrade.

The good news is that many other website builders provide better functionality and features, at a lower cost. In this article, we’ll share our expert pick of the best Wix alternatives that are more powerful and reliable.

Why Are People Switching Away From Wix?

Wix is a popular website builder that’s advertised to have a simple drag and drop builder which makes it easy to build websites quickly.

However, they limit users to specific features, and you will need to pay for every third-party app or plugin to add more options to your site. With the growth of your business, it becomes more expensive to use and still limited in terms of features.

It also limits you in your website template or theme selection. This means you cannot switch to a different template after your website is live on the internet.

With these limitations, Wix is upsetting a lot of users and giving them a reason to switch away to other more powerful and reliable solutions.

Luckily, there are many Wix alternatives with better features, flexibility, and prices.

Best Wix Alternatives

There are multiple website builders that you can choose for your site. We have handpicked the best Wix alternatives for your small businesses, blogs, online stores (eCommerce sites), etc.

Here are the best Wix alternatives that you can use.

1. WordPress

WordPress is the most popular website builder and a perfect alternative to Wix. Over 43% of websites in the world are using WordPress which makes WordPress the de-facto market leader.

There are two different versions of WordPress which often confuses beginners. WordPress.com is a hosted solution (ranked #8 – we’ll talk about it later in this article), and WordPress.org, is a self-hosted and free solution (ranked #1).

For more details, you should check out our complete guide on the difference between WordPress.com vs WordPress.org.

WordPress is very easy to set up and it gives you maximum control of your website. It is highly flexible and allows you to easily start a blog, create an online store, build a community forum, or make any type of site.

It also comes with thousands of WordPress themes, WordPress plugins, and widgets to quickly design your site and add new features to it.

Unlike Wix, it is an open-source website builder with a simple admin panel for customization. You can also use the WordPress customizer with live preview to make changes to your site.

To use WordPress, you’ll need a WordPress hosting platform (where you’ll store your files) and a domain name (the URL that your users will use to browse your site, like wpbeginner.com or google.com) to set up your website.

For more details, see our guide on Wix vs. WordPress.

Price: WordPress is a FREE website builder. But, you need to purchase a domain name and web hosting that costs 14.99/year and $7.99/month, respectively.

Bluehost offers WPBeginner users a huge discount on web hosting, a free domain name, and a free SSL certificate. It is a popular web hosting company, and an official WordPress hosting partner.

This lets you make a WordPress site for as low as $1.99 per month, which is a lot cheaper than Wix or any other website builder in the market.

2. Web.com

Web.com is a popular website builder for small businesses. Because of their affordable pricing plans, they’re an extremely popular Wix alternative amongst business owners.

They offer an easy-to-use drag & drop website builder, so anyone can build a website without needing technical skills.

You can pick from their thousands of beautiful pre-made website templates and customize the design with just point and click. Their eCommerce features make it easy for you to create an online store and accept payments on your website.

All Web.com plans come with a free domain name, business email address, autosave & backup features, website security solution, SEO tools, website analytics, and other powerful website tools.

Price: Web.com pricing starts at $1.95 per month for their starter plan. To create an eCommerce website, you will need their eCommerce plan which starts at $3.95 per month. This is one of the cheapest Wix alternatives in the market that also offers powerful features.

3. HubSpot Website Builder

HubSpot Website Builder is a powerful website builder that’s built for marketers and small business owners. It is an alternative to Wix and lets you set up a custom website in a few minutes, there’s no learning curve or coding involved.

The best part about the HubSpot content management system is that it combines the power of their drag & drop website builder, marketing automation tools, and CRM, so you can deliver a personalized digital experience to your users.

You can use their conversion-optimized website templates to quickly build a website that delivers results. Their drag-and-drop editor makes it easy for you to customize existing templates or make a custom web design from scratch.

HubSpot website builder seamlessly integrates with their CRM platform, so you can leverage the customer data to create a completely personalized digital experience for each website visitor.

Other notable features include SEO tools, powerful analytics tools, blogging tools, live chat software, email marketing feature, multi-language content support, and more.

If we talk about features, HubSpot is one of the most powerful and professional website builders in the market for marketers and small businesses.

Price: HubSpot Website Builder offers a free plan that allows you make a website or blog with the HubSpot branding. Paid plans starts as low as $15 per month.

You get all the powerful marketing features like CRM, email marketing, live chat, forms, popups, analytics, and more.

The good thing is that HubSpot recognizes that WordPress is the most popular website builder, so they have a free WordPress plugin that lets you combine the power of HubSpot’s marketing tools with WordPress, so you get the best of both worlds.

4. Hostinger AI Website Builder

Hostinger AI Website Builder is a smart site builder offered by Hostinger, a popular web hosting company. This Wix alternative lets you create a custom website in just 4 clicks.

Simply select the type of website you want, add a short description of what you need, and the AI (artificial intelligence) builder will create it for you. This includes relevant images and content.

If you don’t want to build your site with AI, Hostinger also provides over 150+ website templates that you can customize using an easy drag and drop editor. You can quickly rearrange elements, change the colors and fonts, and more.

It even comes with mobile editing, which makes it easy to create, customize, and publish your website on-the-go.

Hostinger’s website builder also comes with essential eCommerce features. You can sell up to 500 products with no hidden transaction fees and it integrates with 20+ payment methods.

Price: Hostinger starts at $2.69 per month for the Premium Website Builder plan. It comes with a free domain name, free email address, drag and drop editor, AI website builder, and more.

If you want to create an eCommerce store, you’ll need the Business Website Builder plan, which starts at $3.59 per month.

The Business Website Builder plan also comes with additional AI features like an AI image generator, AI writer, AI heatmaps, and AI SEO tools.

5. SeedProd

SeedProd is an ultimate theme and a drag and drop page builder for WordPress websites. It comes with 300+ website templates for different business niches.

You can use their theme builder to create completely custom WordPress themes without writing any coding knowledge.

It also includes 100+ pre-made professionally designed section templates to help you save time. You can simply pick and choose the best design elements to create any website or online store easily.

With its point-and-click tools, you can start editing any section of your site from the frontend. SeedProd is easy to use, and it gives you complete control of everything, including fonts, colors, shapes, backgrounds, custom CSS and HTML, etc.

It comes with advanced editing features like multi-select and bulk editing, find and replace styles, powerful color manager, copy and paste styles, and more.

Plus, it offers a powerful AI assistant that will instantly generate engaging written content and original images for your website.

SeedProd is better and faster than Wix. And, it combines with WordPress to offer a more robust and powerful solution for building any website, blog, online store, or membership site.

Unlike other Wix alternatives, SeedProd comes built-in with subscriber management, email marketing integrations, dynamic personalization options, proper access control, and more.

Price: The starting price is $39.50 per year with 1 year of premium support and product updates.

To access the theme builder, you can upgrade to the Pro plan which also includes full website kits, 2 million stock photos, email marketing integrations, and more, for $199.50 per year.

Both plans include a risk-free 30-day money-back guarantee (no questions asked). So, you can get familiar with the platform without worrying about your money.

Thrive Theme Builder is another popular drag and drop theme builder for WordPress websites. It lets you easily create a custom, high-converting website, without having to touch a line of code.

Thrive Theme Builder comes packaged with four different website themes that include ready-made web page and landing page templates that you can load with one click.

The templates are designed to look amazing right out of the gate. But, you can easily customize every part of your site, like the header, footer, blog posts, category pages, search pages, and more, simply by pointing and clicking.

It also comes with 100+ design and site-building elements that you can add to your site using the drag and drop functionality. You can choose from pre-made elements like call-to-action buttons, lead generation forms, testimonials, and product highlights, just to name a few.

Thrive Theme Builder seamlessly integrates with WooCommerce so that you can create a beautiful online store. In addition, it connects with many popular email service providers and CRM software to manage your leads.

Price: Thrive Theme Builder is priced at $99 per year for the standalone plugin, which includes unlimited updates and 24/7 customer support.

You can also purchase the Thrive Theme Builder plugin and the Thrive Architect plugin, a drag and drop landing page builder, in a bundle for $199 per year.

Or, you can get the entire Thrive Suite of plugins, which includes Thrive Theme Builder, Thrive Apprentice, Thrive Comments, Thrive Leads, and more, for $299 per year.

7. Beaver Builder

Beaver Builder is a powerful WordPress page builder plugin that combines with other WordPress features to let you launch your website quickly.

It comes with dozens of beautiful landing pages and content page templates that are ready to add to your site. You can replace the demo text and images with your original content and publish the pages.

Unlike Wix, Beaver Builder lets you create fully customizable pages with an intuitive drag and drop builder for the setup. You can also use the WordPress customizer to make changes to your site. To learn more, see our complete Beaver Builder review.

For enterprise users who want white labeling to get full control of the software, they offer an Agency plan for $399.

Beaver Builder also comes with a Beaver Themer addon that allows you to create completely custom WordPress themes from scratch without writing any code. This is great for aspiring developers and / or agencies who want to build websites faster.

Other Wix Alternatives

You may also find other Wix alternatives that are ideal for different types of use cases.

Gator by HostGator

Gator is a leading site builder by HostGator. Since it is built by a popular hosting company, it is an ultimate website builder solution and a perfect alternative to Wix.

It comes with over 200 fully customizable templates to design a website easily. These templates are beautiful and stylish to make a powerful first impression on your website visitors.

Gator is a hosted solution, which means they take care of your website backups, updates, and software. This allows you to focus on the growth of your business.

It also offers multiple tools, social media integration, a live Instagram feed, and support for eCommerce stores. Moreover, it has premium-quality stock photos that you can use on your site.

Price: Gator has premium plans starting from $3.46 per month, making it affordable for small businesses and startups. Each plan comes with a free domain name, free SSL certificate, website analytics, and premium 24/7 live support.

WordPress.com

WordPress.com is a managed hosting solution for WordPress blogs and websites. It is built by Matt Mullenweg, the co-founder of self-hosted WordPress and founder of Automattic.

For more details, you should check out our article on how are WordPress.com and WordPress.org related.

Unlike self-hosted WordPress.org, you don’t need to worry about software updates and backups with WordPress.com. It is a fully custom website builder for beginners and bloggers.

WordPress.com doesn’t come with a drag and drop builder. But you can use the native WordPress live customizer to make changes easily.

Price: It offers a free basic plan with limited features. The personal plan costs $4 per month, including a custom domain, which the free plan doesn’t offer.

Their premium plan for freelancers costs $8 per month. For small business websites, they offer a business plan costing $10 per month for the first year with our coupon code WPB50.

If you want to run an online store with custom features and WooCommerce extensions, then it has an eCommerce plan for $22.50 per month. All their plans are billed annually.

BigCommerce

BigCommerce is a beginner-friendly eCommerce builder for your online store. It is a 1-stop solution to create powerful eCommerce shops and is fully scalable to take your business to the next level.

It is a hosted platform, and its team of experts will take care of your website speed, security, updates, and any other technical issues.

BigCommerce has a strong affiliation with WordPress, which means you can combine the features of the 2 most powerful platforms to set up an excellent eCommerce solution for your users.

When it comes to payment gateways, BigCommerce supports PayPal, Stripe, Apple Pay, Amazon Pay, Chase Pay, Visa Checkout, Square, and more. It doesn’t charge any transaction fees, which means you get the complete profit from your sales.

It is a perfect eCommerce solution for online store owners, and far better than the Wix eCommerce builder.

Price: It offers a 15-day free trial to test all features of the BigCommerce platform. The paid plans start from $29 per month and offer unlimited bandwidth. For more conversion optimization features and eCommerce tools, you can upgrade to a better plan as your business grows.

Shopify

Shopify is an eCommerce software that integrates with your website builder to set up an online store. You can use Shopify as a Wix alternative.

It comes with features like inventory management, automated marketing, 1-click checkouts, easy shipping, and more.

Unlike BigCommerce, you need to pay transaction fees for every sale you make using Shopify. It also becomes expensive when you want to add more features and extensions to grow your online store.

This eCommerce platform also offers an app store where you can download addons to extend the functionality your online store. There’s also a Shopify mobile app that you can use to manage your store from anywhere.

Squarespace

Squarespace is a premium website builder and an alternative to Wix. It is easy to use for beginners to add content anywhere on the site without editing any code.

However, Squarespace has limited third-party integrations, which can be a hurdle in the growth of your business. It can be ideal at the start, but as your business grows, you need to move to a better platform like WordPress.

You can read our complete article on Squarespace vs WordPress for more details.

Weebly

Weebly is a flexible website builder for any type of website. It is an alternative to Wix for a long time and is ideal for small businesses and startups.

It restricts you to limited features and doesn’t let you add third-party tools to set up a powerful site. If you plan to run an online store, Weebly also charges a 3% transaction fee for every payment.

Conclusion: Which is the Best Wix Alternative?

If you are looking for a perfect Wix alternative at an affordable price, then WordPress is the right choice. It is highly flexible, user-friendly, and offers endless customization options.

You can use Bluehost for WordPress websites to get the maximum discounts and start as low as $1.99 per month.

After that you can either use SeedProd, Thrive Architect, or Beaver Builder, which are powerful WordPress drag and drop page builder plugins, to design beautiful website layouts without writing any code.

If you’re looking for a non-WordPress solution that has better support and ease of use than Wix, then we recommend looking at Web.com. It’s a great solution for small business owners looking to build an online presence. Their pricing is very affordable, and they offer expert support including custom website design services.

You can also try the HubSpot website builder. It is perfect for small businesses that are looking for an integrated marketing solution that has email marketing, CRM, sales tools, and more. Because of all the powerful tools, HubSpot is a direct Wix alternative.

In case, you want to build a powerful online store to replace the Wix eCommerce solution, then you’ll need BigCommerce or Shopify. It comes with all the essential features and both offer way more powerful eCommerce solutions than Wix.

Note: We have tried all the top website building software including GoDaddy Website Builder, Google Sites, Jimdo, Webnode, Webflow, and many others. But, we decided not to include them as best alternatives in this list to help you avoid choice paralysis.
